Save Your Memories Forever!

Using advanced Computer Vision technology, Secari helps you efficiently organize your old printed photos and save them digitally on your drive with just a few clicks!

How To Use It

Place Photos in Scanner

Put multiple photos in one scanned image. Leave at least one inch of space between images and the sides.

Scan the Photos

Save the scanned images on your device where you can upload them to the website

Submit the Photos

Click on "GET STARTED" on the homepage and select the images from your system

Download and Save

Once the photos are cropped out, you can download the zip file containing all the photos!

The Creator!

Hi! I'm Matthew, the founder of Secari. I got this inspiration after trying to create a birthday slideshow for my grandaunt. I could not find a solution today that was able to efficiently scan hundreds of her old printed photos from various pages, so I designed this application to speed up the entire process. Secari can scan a standard page of multiple and various size photos and crop them into individual files to be saved forever.
